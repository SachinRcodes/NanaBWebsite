More Than
Just Food.

Nana B's Country Kitchen was born from love — love for family, love for food, and love for the memories made around a table full of great cooking.

Owners Victor Friar and Keysha Nichols-Friar dedicated the restaurant to both of their mothers. Every dish on the menu carries their families' love.

Not a single ingredient comes frozen. Not a single shortcut is taken. Everything is scratch-made from treasured family recipes.

"Come hungry, leave happy. That's the promise we make every single day we open these doors."

Victor & Keysha
Friar

Victor and Keysha Friar bring two family traditions under one roof. Victor brings his legendary wings recipe and signature special sauce. Keysha carries her mother's dessert legacy — especially that unforgettable banana pudding.

Together they've built a place where food is a celebration of where they came from and a gift to the Round Rock community.

The name "Nana B" is a tribute to both of their mothers — the women who started it all.
